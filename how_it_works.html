How It Works

🧑‍⚕️ How Patients or Hospitals Can Use Rakt-Setu

Rakt-Setu helps in emergency situations where blood is urgently needed, especially when it's a rare group, unavailable in blood banks, or in rural areas lacking blood facilities.

Step 1: Click on “Request Urgent Blood” from the navigation bar.

Step 2: Fill in the required details, including blood group, contact number, and accurate location.

Step 3: After submission, an automatic email alert is instantly sent to all matching donors located nearby.

Step 4: You can also click “Find Donors” to manually browse a list of donors filtered by location and blood group, along with their contact details for direct reach-out.

Step 5: Visit “Donor Profiles” to view and appreciate registered donors who are ready to help during emergencies.

🩸 How Blood Donors Can Help

People willing to donate blood in future emergencies can register through Rakt-Setu and be part of our life-saving network.

Step 1: Click on “Register as a Future Donor” from the navigation bar.

Step 2: Submit your details such as name, blood group, contact number, and location.

Step 3: Your profile will be added to our secure donor database.

Step 4: Whenever someone nearby requests your blood group, an email alert will be sent to you to offer help if you're available.

📊 Platform Summary

📢 Request Urgent Blood: Post emergency requests and notify matching donors instantly.

🔍 Find Donors: Search and contact suitable donors filtered by blood group and area.

📇 Donor Profiles: A dedicated section showcasing individuals who have registered to help during emergencies.

🙋 Register as a Future Donor: Become a future donor and be part of the Rakt-Setu network.
